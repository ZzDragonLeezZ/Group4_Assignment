About Us

Vets for Pet Animal Clinic

Vets for Pets Animal Clinic(VPAC)is a well-established,full service, small animal and exotic veterinary clinic providing comprehensive preventive, medical,surgical and dental care.We provide a broad spectrum of diagnostic procedures through in-house testing and the use of external laboratories.We also work closely with local practices when special diagnostic procedures are required
